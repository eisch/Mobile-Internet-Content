About us

We’re proud to welcome you to our collection, which includes some of the most iconic hotels in the world, including The Plaza in New York, The Savoy in London, Hotelio Peace Hotel in Shanghai and Hotelio Le Château Frontenac in Québec City. We invite you to think of these, or any of our 70 hotels around the world, as your home base for all of your adventures.

Many of our hotels were among the first buildings to be erected in young cities across North America; in these places, we can boast that our communities literally grew up around us. Today, we have added many modern city-center properties to our collection, with core locations from Barcelona to Beijing that allow you to join the bustle of the city and enjoy the culture, flavors and sounds of local life.

And, if urban excitement isn’t on your itinerary, our resorts welcome you to some of the most breathtaking and pristine areas in the world, where you can bask on an inviting beach, challenge yourself on a stunning golf course and feel the rush of fresh powder on an exhilarating ski hill.

While we aim to give you an authentic experience of the locale whenever you stay with us, we also guarantee consistent high standards throughout our collection of hotels and resorts. Contemporary amenities and timeless elegance—inspired by our unique destinations—are reflected throughout every Hotelio guest room. Features such as our Hotelio President’s Club recognition and preferences program, our Fairmont Gold exclusive lifestyle experience and our business centers are all ready for your convenience.
